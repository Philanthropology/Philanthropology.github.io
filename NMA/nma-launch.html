The Continue Triangle

The goal of this project was to make a New Media website.
Of the pages we studied in class many of them had dark themes either hidden or in plain sight.
I was inspired by the dark tone of New Media Art projects from the early 2000’s in my own work.
Based off the randomly generated sentence, “Strategy focused on the continue triangle” I began thinking about what the continue triangle might mean.
I came up with the idea that it would be a formula for immortality.
The project takes the viewer throughout a facility where live animals are being held and monitored.
These animals are tested on and eventually used in experiments to create a chimera of regular farm animals.
In concept, the living being shown on the “Demonstration” page is immortal but obviously still in progress. This is the strategy focused on the continue triangle.
